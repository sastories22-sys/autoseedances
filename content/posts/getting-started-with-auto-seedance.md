Auto Seedance is the fastest way to run **bulk AI image and video generation** on Dreamina directly from your browser. In this guide we'll walk through installation, queueing your first batch, and exporting the results.

## Why automate AI generation?

Manually clicking "Generate" hundreds of times kills creative momentum. Auto Seedance turns that loop into a single prompt list you can queue, walk away from, and come back to a finished library.

## Installing the extension

1. Visit the [Extension page](/extension)
2. Click **Install for Chrome** or **Install for Edge**
3. Pin the extension to your toolbar

## Queueing your first batch

Drop a list of prompts into the queue panel, set your aspect ratio, and hit start. Auto Seedance handles throttling automatically so you never get rate-limited.

> Pro tip: Use the CSV import to bring in thousands of prompts from a spreadsheet.

## Next steps

Once you've run your first batch, explore the Library to organize generations, or jump into the [Pricing](/pricing) page to unlock unlimited queues.
