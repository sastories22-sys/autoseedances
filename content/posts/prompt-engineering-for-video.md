Great AI video starts with great prompts. After generating thousands of clips through Auto Seedance, we've distilled the patterns that actually move the needle.

## 1. Subject + Action + Environment

The most reliable base template: **`[subject] [doing action] in [environment], [style]`**.

## 2. Camera language matters

Words like *dolly in*, *tracking shot*, *low angle* dramatically change output quality. AI models are trained on film descriptions — speak their language.

## 3. Lighting is half the prompt

- Golden hour
- Soft window light
- Neon backlight
- Volumetric god rays

## 4. Negative prompts

Tell the model what to avoid: blurry, distorted hands, extra limbs.

## 5. Aspect ratio drives composition

Vertical 9:16 produces tighter close-ups by default. Wide 16:9 invites landscapes.

## 6. Reference real cinematographers

"In the style of Roger Deakins" produces measurably better results than abstract adjectives.

## 7. Iterate in batches

This is where Auto Seedance shines — queue 20 variations of the same prompt and pick the winner.
